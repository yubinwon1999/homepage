Type Specimen for Johnston 100

Instructor_Joseph Newton
Year_2022 Fall

Specimens are basically advertisements for fonts. I wanted to prove why P22 Johnston type face is unique and worth spending money on. I made packaging by exclusively using the typeface. I made patterns or sometimes practical objects by using the typeface. The boldness and clearness seemed like the most crucial feature of this typeface so I tried to focus on readability.
